# Privacy Policy for Jobs4Dent

**Last updated:** November 22, 2025

Jobs4Dent ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Jobs4Dent.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for an account, create a profile, or communicate with us. This may include:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Professional details (dental license, experience, education)
- Profile photograph

### Device and Usage Information

When you access or use our application, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and your usage of our app, including:

- Device type and operating system
- Log data (IP address, access times)
- Location data (if you grant permission)

## Permissions We Request

To provide specific features, we may request access to certain permissions on your device:

- **Camera:** We request camera access to allow you to take and upload a profile picture or scan documents directly within the app.
- **Storage / Photos:** We request access to your device's storage or photo gallery to allow you to upload existing photos or documents (such as resumes or licenses) to your profile.
- **Location:** We request location access to help you find jobs or candidates near your current location.

You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- Create and manage your account and profile.
- Connect dental professionals with job opportunities.
- Process your applications.
- Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **With Other Users:** Your professional profile (excluding sensitive contact details unless you choose to share them) may be visible to other users (e.g., clinics looking for dentists) to facilitate job matching.
- **Service Providers:** We may share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics).
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no security system is impenetrable and we cannot guarantee the security of our systems 100%.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
